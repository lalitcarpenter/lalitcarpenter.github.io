OUR PRODUCTION & QUALITY TEST PROCESS

We at Adva Oxidane are committed to providing our consumers with pure, safe and healthy drinking water.

Hence Adva Oxidane Packaged Drinking Water is our Promise of Purity. Here's a breakdown of our purification process.

Water is passed thru a carbon filter followed by a sand filter to eradicate impurities and bad odours. It is then treated with R.O and the proportions of minerals are controlled to meet the standards of Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), Government of India, Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) is an autonomous body established under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India. Water is then treated UV followed by ozone to get rid of all types of bacteria. It is then bottled and sealed as a fully automated machine process.

We also have a distinguished automatic bottle cleaning process using disinfectant sterilisers, hot water and ozone that is manually inspected and verified.

Every Adva Oxidane Packaged Drinking Water bottle follows various steps of Quality Tests & Purification Processes, so that we can provide you guaranteed purity in every drop……..

  • Water Collection & Purification

    • Collected water undergoes quality checks at source.

  • Ozonisation

    • Ozone kills the harmful bacteria.

  • Sand Filter

    • Removes unwanted particles up to 30 microns.

  • Double Filtration Carbon Filter

    • Eliminate colour, odour & harmful pesticides.

  • Reverse Osmosis

    • Excess minerals and salts are removed.

  • Oxygen Enrichment

    • Here we add back the right amount of Oxygen making our water healthy & giving it a taste of Purity & with added Oxygen.

  • Micron - Filtration

    • This ensures that even the smallest micron-particles (upto 0.45microns) are removed from the water.

  • Careful Bottling

    • Most of our bottles are blown on site to avoid any external contamination. Bhagirath is bottled and packaged using modernized equipment.

  • Quality Control

    • Every bottle has a code printed stating the batch number, date and manufacturing location. Stringent quality control checks are done for each & every batch so that Bhagirath's quality standards are met.
