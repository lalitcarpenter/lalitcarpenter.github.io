Policies & Conservation

Our aims to advance an environmentally and socially responsible Palm Oil & Oil Seed Industry. Key to this Policy is the scope which extends beyond its own mills and plantations to include joint ventures regardless of shareholding and third-party suppliers.

No Deforestation:
  • No Development of High Carbon of High Carbon Stock Forests
  • No Development of High Conservation Areas
  • No Burnings

No Development on Peat
  • Progressively reduce greenhouse gases (GHG) emission on existing plantation
  • Best Management Practices for existing plantations on Peat
  • Where Feasible, explore restoration by working with expert stakeholders and communities

No Exploitation of People and Local Communities
  • Respect and recognise the rights of all workers including contract, temporary and migrant workers
  • Facilitation of inclusion of small holders into the supply chain
  • Free Prior and Informed Consent from indigenous and local communities to operations of lands to which they hold legal, communal or customary rights
  • Resolve all Complaints and conflicts through open, transparent and consultative process.
