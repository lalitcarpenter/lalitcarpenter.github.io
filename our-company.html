Our Company

Our integrated operations provide greater control over traceability, quality and sustainability.

We collaborate with farmers, customers, NGOs, government agencies, foundations and financiers to scale social and environmental programmes.

Our processing, innovation and R&D centre create ingredient formats, blends and recipes to satisfy multiple consumer demands, from taste and aroma, to clean label and convenience.

We are New India's emerging food and agri-business, supplying food ingredients, feed and fibre to thousands of customers worldwide. Also contributes to the society and enviornment by supplying Bio Fuels & Bio Plastics a serious step towards sustainability and circular economy.

Each customer depends on us to provide goods and services that are safe, on-time and consistently in line with the required specification. They also rely on us to help them innovate and create new opportunities.
