Oil Seed Cultivation & Production Program

Our Strategic Model & Methodology of Contract Farming Method with Specific Co-operative Task Forces for Oil Seed Cultivation & Palm Oil Plantation & Other Oil Seed Cultivation with the help of Government Programs, Tri Party Contract Farming Method.

Our Special Policy developed specifically to safeguard the buyer as well as farmers in the "Contract Farming" Scheme

Tripartite Agreement: Three parties viz. Contract Sponsor / Buyer, Farmer and concerned Co-operative shall be signing the Agreement to ensure that all the parties adhere to the terms and conditions of the contract.

Co-operatives as Facilitators: Cooperatives shall act as facilitators and act as a Nodal Centre between the Contract Sponsor / Buyer and the farmer and also directly with the Government for benefits of Farmers with all incentives and schemes.

Technical and financial support: The farmers can get advantage of technical research done by contract sponsor / Buyer and may also get financial support in form of seeds, fertilizer, include harvesting techniques.

This benefit Farmers and Encourage Cultivation and adopt methods to cultivate Higher Yield Oil Seed Crop in turn contribute to Nations Mission to Increase Oil Seed Cultivation.
