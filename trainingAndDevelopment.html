Training & Development Programs

Bhagirath Industries Training & Development Programs are subject specific goal oriented programs which includes Certificate Courses, Internships, Apprentice, Industrial Training’s etc.

Our development programs are designed to expose ambitious minds to the world of Bhagirath Industries and prepare them as our next generation of leaders.

The programs schedule is customised designed to specific goal of training and development program which involves various rotations through the full value chain. Participants work in many different parts of the business in various countries, building their networks and understanding the complexity and variety of our work.

For Job Specific Trainings this goes beyond this they have the chance to drive high-impact commercial projects, and after a year of successful completion of Job Training Program, participants are placed in a front-line management role. It’s a fantastic way to get some early experience of the challenge and empowerment of one of our leadership positions.

These training programmes will test your initiative, stimulate your ambition and creativity, and demand your energy and intellect. It will give you the global exposure, networks, and perspective you need for a high-impact career.
